About Us

Villa Estelita is a private vacation house in Pagsanjan, Laguna — designed for families and groups who want the entire place to themselves. Modern architecture, a serene pool courtyard, and cozy indoor spaces come together for stays that feel both premium and personal.

Whether it is a weekend escape, a birthday celebration, or a team retreat, you get exclusive use of the property — no shared facilities, no crowds, just your group and your time.

House Policies

Deposit: Non-refundable.
Reservations: First come, first served.
Check-in/out: Follow your package schedule. Valid ID required. No waiting area for early arrivals.
Extra person: ₱150 per person above 15 PAX.
Heated jacuzzi: 30 min free use — 21-hour rate only.
Videoke & noise: Off at 10:00 PM (Pagsanjan ordinance).
Gas stove: ₱500 fee — inform caretaker (Airbnb guests exempt).
Bring: Towels, toiletries, kitchen utensils, and charcoal for grilling.
Pets: Allowed — keep outside rooms in cages.
Staff: Last call at 8:00 PM (emergencies excepted).
Pool: Shower before swimming. No running. Adult supervision for children.
Belongings: Keep items inside rooms — not outside or in vehicles.
Occasions: Inform us for birthdays, company outings, or events.
Directions: Search “Villa Estelita Pagsanjan Laguna” on Waze or Google Maps.
